What is XII World Congress?

ISUD's XII World Congress is a confrence to promote dialogue concerning various crucial philosophical issues in today’s world.

The goal of this confrence is to explore the role of philosophy in our complicated times.

When?

July 10-15, 2018

Where?

Pontifical Catholic University of Peru - Lima, Peru
