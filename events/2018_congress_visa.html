How to Obtain a Visa to Enter Peru

Visa requirements for a tourist visa include, but may not be restricted to :

  • Application Form : pdf form

  • Valid Passport

  • Round Trip Ticket

  • Hotel Reservation, Tourist Package Reservation or Invitation Letter

  • Proof of sufficient funds

  • Passport photos

  • Application Fee

  • In some countries: Personal interview

  • In some countries: Proof of legal residency

  • Further information on official website

Country Specific Information :

  • For people that came from China or India: Do not need visa if you have a at least 6 month vigent visa from USA, Canada, Britain, Autralia or Schengen Space; or have permanent resident in those countries.
